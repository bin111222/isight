# Contoura Vision LASIK in Mumbai: Cost, Benefits & Is It Worth It?

If you have been researching LASIK surgery in Mumbai and come across the term "Contoura Vision," you are probably wondering what makes it different — and whether the higher price tag is justified. The short answer: Contoura LASIK in Mumbai is a topography-guided procedure that uses a 22,000-point map of your cornea to personalise the laser treatment beyond what standard LASIK can achieve. For the right patient, it can deliver sharper, cleaner vision than any other flap-based LASIK technique available today.

This guide breaks down exactly how Contoura works, who it helps most, what the research shows, and what it costs at reputable clinics across Mumbai in 2026.

## What Is Contoura Vision LASIK?

Standard LASIK treats your eye based on your spectacle prescription alone — it corrects the sphere and cylinder numbers your optometrist measured. Contoura Vision goes further.

Before surgery, a corneal topographer scans the surface of your eye and captures **22,000 unique elevation data points**. Think of it as a high-resolution terrain map of your cornea. No two corneas are identical; even if two people have the same −4.00 prescription, the microscopic shape irregularities on their corneas are completely different.

Contoura's software analyses these irregularities — called higher-order aberrations (HOAs) — and creates a personalised ablation profile. When the excimer laser fires during your procedure, it does not just correct your refractive error; it simultaneously smooths out every micro-imperfection identified in the topography map.

The result: the optical zone of your cornea becomes more uniform, which translates to sharper daytime vision, reduced night glare, fewer halos around lights, and — in many cases — vision that is actually better than what your best spectacle or contact lens correction ever gave you.

### What Does "22,000 Data Points" Actually Mean?

To put it in perspective: standard LASIK treatment is based on roughly 200 data points from your refraction. Wavefront-guided LASIK captures around 1,200–1,500 data points using an aberrometer. Contoura captures 22,000 data points from corneal topography maps. That is roughly 15–100 times more information, allowing for a level of personalisation that was not possible in earlier-generation procedures.

## Who Benefits Most from Contoura LASIK?

Contoura is not a universal upgrade for everyone — it shines brightest in specific situations. Your surgeon should recommend it after reviewing your topography maps, not as a default upsell.

### Patients with Astigmatism

Astigmatism arises from an irregular corneal shape. Because Contoura maps the actual surface irregularity rather than just treating the measured cylinder, it addresses astigmatism at the corneal level more precisely. Patients with significant or irregular astigmatism often report far less residual blur or ghosting after Contoura compared to standard LASIK.

### Night Drivers and People with Halos or Glare

If you currently see starbursts around headlights or halos around streetlights even with glasses, your cornea likely has higher-order aberrations contributing to those symptoms. Contoura's targeted ablation smooths these out. Multiple peer-reviewed studies confirm superior night-vision outcomes with Contoura versus standard LASIK.

### Higher Spectacle Prescriptions

Patients with prescriptions above −4.00 or −5.00 D tend to have more corneal irregularity. Contoura's detailed map ensures the laser treats the full optical complexity of the cornea, not just the average curvature implied by the prescription.

### Pilots and Professionals with Demanding Visual Standards

Aviation and defence authorities in several countries accept topography-guided LASIK for candidates. The higher probability of achieving 20/15 or better makes Contoura appealing to anyone whose livelihood depends on exceptional visual acuity.

### Patients Who Want the Best Possible Outcome from a Flap-Based Procedure

If you are choosing a flap-based LASIK procedure (as opposed to flapless SMILE or SILK), Contoura represents the current gold standard in Mumbai and globally.

## The Contoura Procedure: Step by Step

The overall time in the laser room is the same as standard LASIK — typically 10–15 minutes per eye. The "extra" work happens before you ever enter the procedure suite.

### Step 1: Advanced Pre-operative Mapping (1–2 Hours Before or on a Separate Visit)

A corneal topographer captures the 22,000-point map of each eye. This data is imported into the laser system's planning software, which generates your personalised ablation profile. Your surgeon reviews the plan, cross-references it with your wavefront and refraction data, and approves the final treatment parameters.

### Step 2: Flap Creation

In Mumbai clinics offering Contoura, the flap is almost always created with a femtosecond laser (bladeless). A thin, precisely dimensioned flap — typically 90–110 microns — is created in under 30 seconds per eye. The flap is gently folded back.

### Step 3: Topography-Guided Excimer Ablation

The excimer laser delivers the personalised Contoura treatment based on your 22,000-point map. An eye-tracker running at 1,050 Hz ensures the laser follows any micro-movement of your eye. Treatment time is approximately 20–40 seconds per eye depending on prescription strength.

### Step 4: Flap Repositioning

The flap is folded back into position. No stitches are required — the flap adheres naturally within minutes through suction.

### Step 5: Immediate Recovery

Your vision will be blurry for a few hours. Most patients in Mumbai notice dramatically improved vision within 24 hours. Full stabilisation takes 4–6 weeks.

## Results: What the Evidence Shows

Contoura Vision has accumulated a robust body of clinical evidence since its FDA approval for topography-guided treatment.

**Key findings from published studies:**

- In the FDA pivotal trial, **30–40% of patients achieved 20/15 or better** uncorrected vision — sharper than the standard "normal" benchmark of 20/20.
- Over 96% of patients achieved 20/20 or better uncorrected distance vision.
- Patients reported statistically significant reductions in glare, halos, and starbursts compared to their pre-operative corrected vision (meaning their vision quality after surgery was better than with glasses before surgery).
- Contoura patients consistently outperformed wavefront-guided and standard LASIK groups on contrast sensitivity tests.

## Is Contoura Worth the Upgrade from Femto LASIK?

This is the most common question patients ask before booking.

**The honest assessment:**

Femto LASIK (bladeless LASIK) is an excellent procedure with a well-established safety record. If your corneal topography shows minimal irregularity and you have a straightforward prescription, Femto LASIK will very likely give you 20/20 vision and you may never notice a difference versus Contoura.

However, Contoura is worth the upgrade if:

- You have any degree of astigmatism
- You currently experience night glare or halos with glasses or contacts
- Your prescription is above −3.00 D or you have irregular topography
- You are a professional (pilot, driver, surgeon, athlete) where visual performance under demanding conditions matters
- You simply want the highest possible probability of achieving the best visual quality a flap-based LASIK can deliver

The price difference between Femto LASIK and Contoura in Mumbai is approximately **₹20,000–₹40,000 per eye**. Given that this is a one-time, permanent vision correction — and that the improvement in visual quality can be meaningful and lasting — most patients who qualify for Contoura consider the upgrade worthwhile.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Contoura LASIK?

Contoura LASIK (also called topography-guided LASIK) is a type of flap-based laser vision correction that uses 22,000 corneal data points to create a personalised laser treatment plan. It corrects your refractive error and simultaneously smooths corneal irregularities that standard LASIK misses, often resulting in vision sharper than your best spectacle correction.

### Contoura vs SMILE: which is better?

They solve different problems. Contoura is a flap-based procedure that excels at treating higher-order aberrations and delivering the best optical quality among flap-based LASIK options. SMILE is flapless, causes less post-operative dry eye, and is better suited to patients at risk of flap complications or those who play contact sports. SMILE does not offer the topography-guided personalisation that Contoura provides. Your surgeon should recommend the right procedure after reviewing your corneal maps.

### What results can I expect from Contoura LASIK?

Over 96% of Contoura patients achieve 20/20 uncorrected vision. Approximately 30–40% achieve 20/15 — better than the standard "normal." Night vision outcomes are superior to standard LASIK, with significant reductions in halos and glare.

Contoura Vision LASIK costs ₹75,000–₹1,00,000 per eye at reputable clinics in Mumbai in 2026. Both eyes typically cost ₹1,50,000–₹2,00,000 as a package. EMI options are available at most centres.
