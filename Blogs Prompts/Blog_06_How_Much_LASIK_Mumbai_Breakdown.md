## How to Tell If a Quote Is All-In or Missing Items

When you receive a LASIK quote in Mumbai, ask the clinic to confirm — in writing — whether the following are included:

- [ ] Pre-op corneal mapping (Pentacam / topography)
- [ ] Dry eye assessment (Schirmer's test, TBUT)
- [ ] Cycloplegic refraction and dilated retina exam
- [ ] All post-operative medications (antibiotic, steroid, lubricant drops)
- [ ] Minimum four follow-up visits (Day 1, Week 1, Month 1, Month 3)
- [ ] Enhancement / re-treatment within 12 months if needed

If any of these receive a "that's extra" answer, factor the additional cost into your comparison. A seemingly cheaper headline price often becomes more expensive than an all-inclusive quote once you total the full episode.

## North Mumbai vs South Mumbai vs Navi Mumbai: Price Differences

Mumbai is not a monolithic market. Location influences cost in two ways: operating overhead for the clinic, and patient demographic mix.

### South Mumbai (Marine Lines, Colaba, Churchgate)
- Higher clinic overheads; older, established refractive centres
- Prices slightly above Mumbai average
- Surgeons often have very long tenures; strong reputation value
- Approximate premium over base: 10–20%

### Central and Western Suburbs (Andheri, Bandra, Malad, Borivali)
- Largest cluster of mid-tier to premium refractive clinics in Mumbai
- Most competitive pricing; highest volume
- Wide range — from budget to premium available within a few kilometres
- Prices broadly in line with Mumbai averages listed above

### Navi Mumbai (Vashi, Kharghar, CBD Belapur)
- Lower operating overhead than island city and western suburbs
- Procedures typically 10–15% below equivalent quality clinics in Bandra or Andheri
- Fewer premium-tier options but solid mid-range clinics available
- Suitable for patients in Thane or Navi Mumbai to avoid travel

For a ₹1,40,000 procedure quoted in Bandra, an equivalent clinic in Navi Mumbai might quote ₹1,20,000–₹1,25,000 — a genuine saving worth factoring in if you are local to that area.

## FAQ

### How much is LASIK in Mumbai?

LASIK in Mumbai costs ₹20,000–₹1,10,000 per eye depending on the procedure type. For both eyes, the all-in total (including pre-op, medications, and follow-ups) ranges from approximately ₹45,000 for standard LASIK at a budget clinic to ₹2,20,000+ for premium flapless SILK or SMILE at a top-tier centre.

### What is the total cost of LASIK in Mumbai?

The total cost of LASIK in Mumbai for a complete treatment episode — procedure, pre-op workup, medications, and four follow-up visits — is typically ₹52,000–₹85,000 for standard LASIK, ₹1,20,000–₹1,80,000 for Bladeless LASIK, and ₹1,80,000–₹2,20,000 for SMILE or SILK. Always get an itemised written quote.

### What does LASIK for both eyes cost in Mumbai?

Both-eyes LASIK in Mumbai is generally priced at about 10% less than double the per-eye price. A ₹80,000-per-eye SMILE procedure would typically be quoted at ₹1,44,000–₹1,55,000 for both eyes combined. Confirm the all-inclusive total at your consultation.

### Is LASIK cheaper in Navi Mumbai or the suburbs?

Generally yes — clinics in Navi Mumbai and the outer western suburbs of Mumbai operate with lower overheads and may price procedures 10–15% below equivalent clinics in Bandra, Andheri, or South Mumbai. Quality ranges from good to excellent; the key is to verify surgeon qualifications and laser platform vintage regardless of location.

### Can I pay for LASIK in Mumbai by EMI?

Yes. No-cost EMI for 3 to 24 months is available at most major Mumbai LASIK clinics via Bajaj Finserv, HDFC, Pine Labs, and ZestMoney. There is no interest on qualifying no-cost plans. Approval is typically processed within 24 hours with standard KYC documents.
