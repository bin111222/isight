## LASIK Eye Surgery Cost in Mumbai: The Short Answer

LASIK eye surgery in Mumbai costs between **₹20,000 and ₹1,10,000 per eye** in 2026. For both eyes, most Mumbai patients pay somewhere between **₹45,000 and ₹2,20,000 total**, depending on the technology used, the surgeon's experience, and what the clinic includes in the package.

If you're looking for a single headline number: **Standard LASIK in Mumbai averages around ₹25,000–₹40,000 per eye, while premium bladeless options like Contoura, SMILE and SILK sit between ₹70,000 and ₹1,10,000 per eye.**

The rest of this guide tells you exactly what you're paying for, how to judge whether a quote is fair, and how to protect yourself from the two most common problems Mumbai patients report: hidden add-ons and "too-cheap-to-be-true" packages that skip essential pre-op tests.

## What Factors Drive LASIK Price in Mumbai?

Before you compare quotes from two clinics, understand *why* one can be ₹30,000 cheaper than the other for what looks like the same procedure.

### 1. The laser platform
A 2024-generation femtosecond laser (Visumax 800, WaveLight Plus, Teneo) costs 3–4× what an older Allegretto platform costs to buy and maintain. Clinics that have invested in new hardware charge more — and generally deliver faster, more precise results.

### 2. Surgeon experience and case volume
A surgeon who has done 25,000+ LASIK procedures charges more than one who has done 500. In Mumbai, the top 10 refractive surgeons charge an additional ₹10,000–₹25,000 per eye above the clinic's "base" LASIK price.

### 3. Clinic location within Mumbai
South Mumbai (Colaba, Fort, Nariman Point) and Bandra clinics typically sit at the top of the price range. Andheri, Mulund, Chembur and Navi Mumbai centres are often 15–25% cheaper for the same procedure.

### 4. Depth of pre-operative workup
A thorough LASIK workup (topography, pachymetry, dry eye scan, cycloplegic refraction, pupil size under mesopic conditions, posterior segment evaluation) takes 90–120 minutes and involves ₹3,000–₹6,000 worth of consumables. Clinics that skip these to advertise a ₹15,000 LASIK price are cutting the one thing you should never cut.

### 5. Post-operative care inclusions
A good LASIK package in Mumbai includes at least **3–4 follow-up visits** across 6 months plus 4–6 weeks of medication. Some low-cost packages charge these separately — which can add ₹3,000–₹8,000 once you're already committed.

### 6. Enhancement / re-treatment policy
Around 1 in 500 LASIK patients needs a small touch-up within the first year. Premium clinics include this free; budget clinics may charge ₹20,000–₹40,000 for the enhancement.

## What Should a Good LASIK Package in Mumbai Include?

Before you pay a single rupee, confirm in writing that your package includes **every** item in this checklist. If a line item is missing, ask whether it's billed separately and how much.

- [ ] Full pre-op eligibility workup: topography, pachymetry, dry eye assessment, wavefront/aberrometry, pupillometry, dilated retina check
- [ ] Cycloplegic refraction (the "final glass number" test — different from a standard eye test)
- [ ] Named laser platform (not just "advanced laser" — ask which make and model)
- [ ] Surgery itself, for both eyes, same day
- [ ] Surgeon's fees (not just the clinic's fees — some Mumbai clinics separate them)
- [ ] All post-op medications for 4–6 weeks (antibiotic, steroid, lubricating drops)
- [ ] A minimum of 3 scheduled follow-up visits (Day 1, Week 1, Month 1, and ideally Month 3 and 6)
- [ ] Written enhancement policy covering the first 12 months
- [ ] Emergency contact access for 48 hours post-op

## Is Cheap LASIK Safe? How to Evaluate Low-Cost Quotes

Short answer: cheap LASIK *can* be safe — but only if the clinic has lowered cost by scaling volume, not by cutting quality. Here's the difference.

**Safe reasons a clinic can charge less:**
- High surgical volume that amortises equipment costs
- Clinic is in a lower-rent neighbourhood
- The surgeon has just started independent practice but is well-trained
- The laser platform is older but still FDA-approved and well-maintained

**Unsafe reasons a clinic may charge less — walk away:**
- The pre-op assessment is under 30 minutes
- The surgeon meets you only on surgery day (not at the consultation)
- The clinic can't name the specific laser platform they use
- No written enhancement policy
- "Follow-ups at extra cost"
- A single "package" price that won't itemise what's included
- Pressure to book same-day with "limited slot" urgency

A useful rule of thumb in Mumbai: **any LASIK quote below ₹18,000 per eye deserves extra scrutiny**, and any bladeless / SMILE / Contoura / SILK quote below ₹50,000 per eye almost certainly excludes something.

## Does Insurance Cover LASIK in Mumbai?

In most cases, **no**. LASIK is classified as an *elective* refractive procedure by Indian health insurers, which means standard mediclaim, employer group cover and most retail health policies don't cover it.

A few narrow exceptions exist:
- **Occupational necessity:** Some group policies for defence personnel, police, airlines and railways cover LASIK if the job requires spectacle-free vision.
- **High refractive error with medical justification:** Very rarely, insurers may partially reimburse if your power is so high that glasses can't safely correct it — this almost always requires pre-authorisation and a detailed medical recommendation.
- **Corporate wellness top-ups:** A handful of Mumbai corporates (IT, BFSI) include LASIK in premium wellness add-ons. Check with HR.

Always call your insurer directly *before* booking. Ask for a written confirmation on whether "laser refractive surgery (CPT/procedure code 65760/65765/66999)" is covered. Don't rely on a verbal "yes".

## How to Get an Accurate LASIK Cost Estimate in Mumbai

A phone quote or WhatsApp "price list" is not a real estimate — because your actual price depends on things that can only be measured in person:

1. **Your refractive power** — very high myopia or hyperopia rules out some cheaper procedures.
2. **Corneal thickness (pachymetry)** — thin corneas may need SMILE or Contoura rather than standard LASIK.
3. **Tear film status** — significant dry eye may require pre-LASIK management that adds to cost.
4. **Pupil size** — larger pupils sometimes need wavefront-optimised treatment (slightly more expensive, far fewer night halos).
5. **Astigmatism and higher-order aberrations** — drive whether Contoura is worth the upgrade.

The only reliable way to know what **you** will pay: a full pre-LASIK assessment. At reputable Mumbai clinics, this takes 90–120 minutes and is usually charged at ₹1,500–₹3,500 — credited back if you proceed with surgery.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does LASIK cost in Mumbai for both eyes?
Most Mumbai patients pay between ₹45,000 and ₹2,20,000 total for both eyes, depending on the procedure. Standard LASIK both eyes averages ₹50,000–₹80,000; bladeless/Contoura both eyes averages ₹1,40,000–₹2,00,000; SMILE and SILK both eyes typically ₹1,60,000–₹2,20,000.

### What is the cheapest LASIK in Mumbai?
The cheapest credible LASIK in Mumbai is **Standard LASIK at ₹20,000–₹25,000 per eye** at high-volume suburban centres. Anything below ₹18,000 per eye usually excludes pre-op testing or follow-ups — ask carefully.

### Is LASIK covered by insurance in India?
Standard health insurance in India does not cover LASIK because it's classified as elective. Exceptions exist for certain defence, airline, and corporate policies, and very rarely for medically justified high refractive errors. Always get written confirmation from your insurer before booking.

### What is the cost of Contoura LASIK in Mumbai?
Contoura Vision LASIK in Mumbai costs ₹75,000–₹1,00,000 per eye in 2026, or roughly ₹1,40,000–₹1,90,000 for both eyes. The price reflects the 22,000-point corneal mapping and topography-guided laser treatment, which typically delivers sharper low-light and night vision than standard LASIK.

### What is the cost of SMILE LASIK in Mumbai?
SMILE LASIK in Mumbai costs ₹80,000–₹1,05,000 per eye, with both eyes typically ₹1,60,000–₹2,00,000. SMILE is flapless and is often preferred for high myopia, dry-eye-prone patients, and athletes.

### Is it worth paying extra for bladeless / Femto LASIK?
For most Mumbai patients with adequate corneal thickness, bladeless LASIK offers a measurably lower flap-complication rate (roughly 1 in 10,000 vs. 1 in 1,000 for microkeratome), faster recovery in the first 48 hours, and more predictable flap geometry. If your budget allows, the upgrade from Standard to Femto is one of the highest-value decisions in the LASIK pricing ladder.

### Can I pay for LASIK on EMI in Mumbai?
Yes. Almost every established LASIK clinic in Mumbai offers No-Cost EMI for 3–12 months via Bajaj Finserv, HDFC, Pine Labs or ZestMoney. Tenures up to 24 months are available but may carry 10–14% APR.

### How much is LASIK surgery in Navi Mumbai?
LASIK in Navi Mumbai (Vashi, Nerul, Kharghar, Belapur) is typically 10–20% cheaper than South Mumbai for the same procedure, mainly because of lower clinic rent. Standard LASIK in Navi Mumbai averages ₹22,000–₹35,000 per eye; Contoura averages ₹65,000–₹85,000 per eye.

### Will the price change on the day of surgery?
It shouldn't. A good Mumbai LASIK clinic gives you a written, itemised quote after your pre-op assessment, and that quote is the final price — barring procedure changes you've approved in writing. If a clinic adds charges on surgery day, push back.
